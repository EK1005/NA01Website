Saving Tips

Tip of the Day

Turn off appliances at the wall when not in use. This reduces standby power and lowers electricity costs.

Use LED bulbs instead of incandescent bulbs.

Wash clothes in cold water whenever possible.

Set air conditioning to 24°C during summer.

Only run your dishwasher when it is full.

Unplug chargers when they are not being used.

Open curtains during the day to use natural light.

Turn lights off when leaving a room.

Use ceiling fans before turning on air conditioning.

Keep refrigerator doors closed as much as possible.

Weekly Energy Saving Challenge

  • Turn off lights when leaving a room.
  • Reduce shower time by 2 minutes.
  • Use fans before air conditioning.
  • Track your electricity usage every day.
  • Unplug devices that are not being used.
